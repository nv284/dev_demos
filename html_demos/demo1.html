Evolution and classification

The first classification of birds was developed by Francis Willughby and John Ray in their 1676 volume Ornithologiae.[8] Carl Linnaeus modified that work in 1758 to devise the taxonomic classification system currently in use.[9] Birds are categorised as the biological class Aves in Linnaean taxonomy. Phylogenetic taxonomy places Aves in the clade Theropoda
